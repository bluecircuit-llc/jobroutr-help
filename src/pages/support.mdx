## Common Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I create an account?**
Visit our [Create User Account](/docs/onboarding/create-user-account) guide to sign up and get started with jobRoutr.

**How do I set up my organization?**
Follow our step-by-step [Create Organization](/docs/onboarding/create-organization) guide to configure your business settings.

**Can I invite my team members?**
Yes! Check out [Invite Your Team](/docs/onboarding/invite-team) to learn how to add technicians and staff to your account.

### Integration & Setup

**Does jobRoutr integrate with QuickBooks?**
Yes! jobRoutr seamlessly integrates with QuickBooks Online. See our [Connect QuickBooks](/docs/onboarding/connect-quickbooks) guide for setup instructions.

**How do I configure email notifications?**
Visit our [Configure Email](/docs/onboarding/configure-email) documentation to set up automated customer notifications.

**What mobile devices are supported?**
jobRoutr works on iOS and Android smartphones and tablets. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play.

### Using the App

**How do technicians view their routes?**
Technicians can view and navigate their optimized daily routes directly in the mobile app.

**Can technicians complete jobs offline?**
Yes! The mobile app works offline and syncs data when an internet connection is available.

**How do I add photos to a service?**
Technicians can capture and attach photos directly within the job details screen in the mobile app.

### Billing & Account

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) securely processed through Stripe.

**Can I change my subscription plan?**
Yes, you can upgrade or change your plan at any time from your account settings.

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes, there is a 30 day free trial period.

## Technical Requirements

### Mobile App
- **iOS:** Requires iOS 15.0 or later
- **Android:** Requires Android 8.0 or later
- Internet connection required for initial setup and data sync

### Web Dashboard
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Stable internet connection

### QuickBooks Integration
- QuickBooks Online subscription
- Admin access to your QuickBooks account
