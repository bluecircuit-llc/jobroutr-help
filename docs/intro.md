# Welcome to JobRoutr

Welcome to JobRoutr, the all-in-one pool service management software designed to streamline your operations, optimize routing, and grow your business. Whether you're a solo operator or managing a team of technicians, JobRoutr helps you work smarter, not harder.

## What is JobRoutr?

JobRoutr is a comprehensive service management platform that helps pool service professionals:

- **Schedule & Route** - Optimize service routes for maximum efficiency
- **Manage Customers** - Keep all customer information and service history in one place
- **Track Jobs** - Monitor job status in real-time from dispatch to completion
- **Mobile Access** - Empower technicians with a powerful mobile app
- **Automate Billing** - Integrate with QuickBooks for seamless invoicing
- **Communicate Better** - Send automated service updates to customers
- **Scale Operations** - Grow your business with tools that scale with you

## Key Features

### Mobile-First Design
Technicians use the intuitive mobile app to view routes, complete tasks, upload photos, and collect customer signatures - all from their smartphone.

### Smart Routing
Optimize daily routes automatically to save time, reduce fuel costs, and serve more customers efficiently.

### QuickBooks Integration
Seamlessly sync customers, products, and invoices with QuickBooks Online for accurate accounting.

### Task Management
Create standardized service checklists to ensure quality and consistency across all jobs.

### Customer Communication
Automatically notify customers when services are scheduled, in progress, or completed.


## Getting Started

New to JobRoutr? Follow our step-by-step guide to get your account set up and ready to go!

### Quick Start: Create Your Account & Complete 5-Step Onboarding

Get up and running with JobRoutr in just a few minutes:

**First:** **[Create Your Account](/docs/onboarding/create-user-account)** - Sign up and verify your email

**Then complete the 5-step onboarding wizard:**

1. **[Create Organization](/docs/onboarding/create-organization)** - Set up your company details and billing information
2. **[Connect QuickBooks](/docs/onboarding/connect-quickbooks)** - Integrate with QuickBooks Online (optional but recommended)
3. **[Build Your Services](/docs/onboarding/build-service)** - Define your service offerings and task lists
4. **[Configure Email](/docs/onboarding/configure-email)** - Set up automated customer communications
5. **[Invite Team Members](/docs/onboarding/invite-team)** - Add your technicians and staff

:::tip Start Here!
If you're brand new to JobRoutr, **[begin with creating your account](/docs/onboarding/create-user-account)** and then follow the 5-step onboarding wizard.
:::

## What You'll Need

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A valid business email address
- Your company information (name, address, phone)
- QuickBooks Online account (optional, but recommended for accounting integration)
- List of services you offer with pricing
- Team member email addresses (if inviting staff)
- Company logo (optional, for email branding)

## Estimated Setup Time

- **Basic Setup** (Steps 1-2): ~5 minutes
- **QuickBooks Integration** (Step 3): ~5 minutes
- **Service Configuration** (Step 4): ~10-20 minutes (depends on number of services)
- **Email Configuration** (Step 5): ~5 minutes
- **Team Invitations** (Step 6): ~5 minutes

**Total Time: 30-40 minutes** to complete full onboarding

:::info Take Your Time
You don't need to complete all steps at once. You can save your progress and return anytime. However, completing all steps ensures you get the most out of JobRoutr from day one.
:::
